In an era where technology continues to advance at an unprecedented rate, the rise of AI-generated deepfakes has become a cause for concern. Deepfakes are hyper-realistic digital forgeries created using artificial intelligence, often blending existing images and videos to produce fabricated content that can be difficult to distinguish from reality. The implications of deepfakes are far-reaching and pose serious threats to individuals, businesses, and even national security. From fake news and misinformation to identity theft and fraud, the potential for harm is vast. Imagine a world where anyone can be portrayed saying or doing anything, regardless of the truth. One of the most terrifying aspects of AI-generated deepfakes is their ability to manipulate public opinion and sow discord. With the power to create convincing videos of public figures saying things they never actually said, deepfakes have the potential to disrupt political processes, incite violence, and undermine trust in institutions.
